At Lokales, we are passionate about sharing our love for sports. We specialize in introducing people to skateboarding, climbing, parkour, and other activities. We believe that the key to a healthy lifestyle is regular and consistent practice. Here, we encourage you to discover new sports, surrounded by a community of like-minded individuals in an inspiring natural environment.

You can easily book your favorite activities online through our website. Simply select the activity, choose your preferred date and time, and complete your booking. You can also use our Exclusive Point Packs to secure your spot.

Exclusive Point Packs are special bundles that allow you to purchase multiple sessions or activities at a discounted rate. Simply buy the pack and redeem points when booking.

Yes, you can usually bring a friend. However, please check the activity description or contact our team to confirm if additional guests are allowed.

Our two Lokales branches offer similar classes and activities but differ in location, facility size, and some specialized offerings. Check our website for more details.

Most of our classes and activities are beginner-friendly. Instructors will guide you through the basics, so no prior experience is necessary in most cases.

You can cancel or reschedule your booking as long as you do so within the time frame specified by our cancellation policy. Please review our terms or contact us for assistance.
